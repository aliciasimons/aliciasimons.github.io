The move away from the graphic of the weapon to a toy comes after mass demonstrations by students in the United States and globally, demanding tighter

control laws. In March, tens of thousands of people gathered across the US at March For Our Lives rallies, led by the student survivors of the Parkland, Florida, shooting. Seventeen people were killed in the attack at Marjory Stoneman Douglas High School.
